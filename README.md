# Epta_Capstone
Drawing on 14 years of ridership data, enrollment trends, and a 445-respondent student survey, this presentation documents the scope, utilization, and student impact of shuttle bus services provided by the Eastern Panhandle Transit Authority to Shepherd University — and makes the case for their continued funding

Final presentation can be viewed here: https://github.com/meekerwv-boop/Epta_Capstone/blob/main/EPTA/EPTA_Funding_Case_2025.pptx

Analysis based on ridership numbers provided by EPTA, University enrollment statistics from the Shepherd University office of Institutional Research, and the results of a student survey and focus group efforts by the Shepherd University Student Affairs division. 

Initial data cleaning conducted in Excel, merges and analysis using Python in a Jupyter Notebook, and visualizations made in Python and within PowerPoint. Each stage is preserved in this repository.
